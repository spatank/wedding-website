E-Visa (Recommended for Tourists)

An e-Tourist Visa is the easiest option for US citizens attending the wedding. It's processed online and typically approved within 3-5 business days.

Key Details:

  • Validity: 30 days from date of arrival
  • Entries: Double entry permitted
  • Cost: Approximately $25-40 USD
  • Processing Time: 3-5 business days (apply at least 2 weeks in advance)

⚠️ Important: Your passport must be valid for at least 6 months from your date of arrival in India.
