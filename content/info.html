Tips for Handling Money:

  • ATMs: Widely available in cities. Use ATMs inside banks for safety. Your US debit card will work (check foreign transaction fees with your bank).
  • Credit Cards: Accepted at hotels and major restaurants, but many smaller vendors are cash-only.
  • Currency Exchange: Best rates at banks or official exchange counters. Avoid street exchanges. Hotels also exchange currency but at a premium.
  • Notify Your Bank: Inform your bank you'll be traveling to India to avoid card blocks. You may also need to activate PINs for your credit cards but tap-to-pay generally works.
  • Cash on Hand: Keep small bills (‚Çπ10, ‚Çπ20, ‚Çπ50) for tips, street food, auto-rickshaws.
