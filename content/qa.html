What are some cultural do's and don'ts?

Do:

  • Remove shoes when entering the mandap, homes, and temples
  • Accept food and drinks when offered (refusing can be seen as impolite)
  • Use your right hand for eating and giving/receiving items
  • Join in the dancing and celebrations!

Don't:

  • Don't touch people's heads or point feet at others (considered disrespectful)
  • Don't use flash photography during religious ceremonies (ask first)
  • Don't expect punctuality - "Indian Standard Time" is real! Events may start 30-60 min late
